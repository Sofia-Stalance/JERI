Why Adopt From JERI?

Save a Life

Every adoption opens space for us to rescue another animal in need.

Health Guarantee

All pets are spayed/neutered, vaccinated, and microchipped before adoption.

Lifetime Support

We provide post-adoption support and will always take our animals back if needed.

The Adoption Difference

When you adopt from JERI, you're not just getting a pet - you're becoming part of our rescue family. We take the time to understand your lifestyle and match you with the perfect companion.

Our animals receive comprehensive behavioral assessments, so you'll know exactly what to expect. We're transparent about any special needs or requirements.
