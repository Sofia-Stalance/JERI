Emergency Animal Rescue

If you've found an animal in immediate danger, we're here to help 24/7.

Our emergency response team can assist with:

  • Injured or sick stray animals
  • Animals in life-threatening situations
  • Wildlife emergencies
  • Animal cruelty cases
Call Now Email Emergency
Emergency Rescue Tips
  • Approach injured animals cautiously
  • Keep them warm and quiet
  • Don't offer food unless instructed
  • Note the exact location found
  • Take photos if safe to do so
