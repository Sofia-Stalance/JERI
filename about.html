About Joint Effort Rescue Initiative (JERI)

At JERI, we believe every animal deserves a second chance at a loving, forever home. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome abandoned, neglected, and abused animals, providing them with the care and compassion they need to thrive.

Our Vision

To create a world where every animal is treated with kindness, respect, and dignity, and where no animal is left homeless or suffering. We envision a community where responsible pet ownership is the norm and where adoptions from shelters are the first choice.

Our Mission

  • Rescue: Actively saving animals from dangerous situations, including abandonment, abuse, and neglect.
  • Rehabilitate: Providing comprehensive medical care, behavioral training, and a safe, nurturing environment for all rescued animals.
  • Rehome: Finding suitable, loving, and permanent homes for our animals through a thorough adoption process.
  • Educate: Raising public awareness about responsible pet ownership, the importance of spaying/neutering, and animal welfare issues.

Why Adopt from JERI?

When you adopt from JERI, you're not just giving an animal a home; you're becoming a part of their incredible journey of recovery and hope. Our animals are thoroughly vetted, vaccinated, spayed/neutered, and receive individualized care to prepare them for their new lives. You'll gain a loyal companion and the satisfaction of knowing you've saved a life.
