Why Adopt?

Behind every shelter door is a heart longing for a home, a soft bed, and someone to call their own. By adopting, you're not just getting a pet; you're offering them a chance at a happy ending.

At JERI, we believe every animal deserves a loving home. Our adoption process is designed to create perfect matches that last a lifetime. When you adopt from us, you're not just saving one life - you're making space for us to rescue another animal in need.

Our animals come from various backgrounds - some were abandoned, others surrendered, and many rescued from dangerous situations. Each receives comprehensive medical care, behavioral assessment, and plenty of love before being made available for adoption.
